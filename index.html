Services

  • Powder Coating
  • Sand/Grit Blasting
  • Pre-Treatment
  • Finishing & QC
  • Transport

  • We have 3 powder coating lines, the biggest of which is 7.6 metres; all are kitted out with the latest Gema Powder Coating system. We run both day and night shifts, and won't be beaten on turnaround time and quality of service.

    • Up to 7.5 m x 3.0 m x 2.2 m (24'7" x 9'10" x 7'2").
    • Up to 3 tonnes (3,000 kg).
    • We mainly powder coat in architectural grade polyester, but also offer pure epoxy, anti microbial powders, Plascoat and nylon powders.
    • Special effects powders; metallic, leatherette, hammer finish and fluorescents.

  • Sand/grit blasting removes rust, existing paint/powder coating and provides an ideal key for powder to attach.

    • Up to 6.0 m x 3.0 m x 2.2 m (19'8" x 9'10" x 7'2").
    • Up to 3 tonnes (3,000 kg).
    • In chilled iron (for aggressive cleaning) or glass/sand (for gentler cleaning on metals such as aluminium).

  • A variety of pre-treatments allow us to safely remove any contaminants or unwanted material that may affect the finish quality.

    • Non-Chromate Conversion Coating (NCCC): an alternative when bulk anodizing is impractical or not cost effective. NCCC leaves the aluminium finish intact, providing additional corrosion protection whilst maintaining the aluminium finish, free of cracking on the surface coating.
    • Degreasing: The process of removing grease and oils from metal which was applied during the construction process.
    • 'T-Washing': A pre-treatment for galvanised steel to ensure the subsequent adhesion of powder.
    • 'Etching': A layer of acid eats into metal and creates a rough area which is more ideal to coat on.
    • 'Tak Ragging': The removal of small dust particles from the metal.
    • De-Gassing: A method used to drive out gas trapped within galvanised steel (alternatively we have powders which do not allow gassing to occur).
    • Sand Blasting: If a product we receive has paint, rust or any other coating which prevents us from producing a perfect finish then sometimes blasting is used.

  • Our quality control ensures every job is finished to the highest standards and packaging options mean it reaches you in the same condition.

    • A Micron Counter can be used to check the thickness of powder.
    • Specialist cling film and/or bubble wrap to ensure the product is delivered scratch free.
    • Items can be securely palletised or packaged to any specification.
    • We have several vehicles suitable of transporting up to 7.5 tonnes (7,500 kg) offering collection and/or delivery.
    • We can collect and deliver to anywhere in Great Britain.
