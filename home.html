Company Bio

Founded in 1995 and based in Glossop, Garie Bevan Coatings Ltd. provides a shot blasting and powder coating service across the North West of England.

We've constantly expanded throughout the years; we now have 3 powder coating lines, 2 blast cabinets and have an extension currently being built to house our new pre-treatment plant.

ISO 9001 registered.

Powder Coating

Size: We can powder coat items up to 7.5 m x 3.0 m x 2.2 m (24'7" x 9'10" x 7'2").

Weight: We can powder coat items up to 1 tonne (1,000 kg).

Powders: We mainly powder coat in architectural grade polyester, but also offer pure epoxy, anti microbial powders, plascoat and nylon powders.

Effects: Incorporated into this range are the special effects powders. These range from metallics, leatherette, hammer finish and fluorescents.

Sand/Grit Blasting

Size: We can sand blast items up to 6.0 m x 3.0 m x 2.2 m (19'8" x 9'10" x 7'2").

Weight: We can sand blast items up to 1 tonne (1,000 kg).

Sands: We blast in chilled iron (for aggressive cleaning) or glass/sand (for gentler cleaning).

Pre-Treatment

Degreasing: The process of removing grease and oils from metal which was applied during the construction process.

'T-Washing':- A pre-treatment for galvanised steel to ensure the subsequent adhesion of powder.

'Etching': A layer of acid eats into metal and creates a rough area which is more ideal to coat on.

'Tak Ragging': The removal of small dust particles from the metal.

De-Gassing: A method used to drive out gas trapped within galvanised steel (alternatively we have powders which do not allow gassing to occur).

Sand Blasting: If a product we receive has paint, rust or any other coating which prevents us from producing a perfect finish then sometimes blasting is used.

Transport

Weight: We have several vehicles suitable of transporting up to 7.5 tonnes (7,500 kg) offering collection and/or delivery.

Coverage: We can collect and deliver to anywhere in Great Britain.
